Indeependence Memorial Hall
Colombo

Independence Memorial Hall is a prominent monument built to commemorate the country’s independence from British rule in 1948. The hall features a striking architectural design reminiscent of traditional Sri Lankan style, with an open-air pavilion, intricate carvings, and statues of national heroes, serving as a significant site for national celebrations and ceremonies.

Henerathgoda Botanical Garden
Gampaha

Henerathgoda Botanical Garden is a picturesque botanical garden established in 1876. Spanning 28 acres, it features a diverse collection of plants, including tropical and subtropical species, along with beautiful walking paths, ornamental ponds, and various themed gardens, making it a serene spot for nature lovers and tourists.

Richmond Castle
Kalutara

Richmond Castle is a stunning mansion built in the late 19th century by a British planter named Edward Barnes. The castle showcases a blend of Gothic and Indo-Saracenic architectural styles, featuring intricate woodwork, beautiful gardens, and historical significance, making it a popular tourist attraction..

Lotus Tower
colombo

The Lotus Tower is a 350-meter tall telecommunications and observation tower in Colombo, Sri Lanka, and the tallest structure in South Asia. Its unique lotus-shaped design symbolizes purity and beauty, and it features a revolving restaurant, exhibition spaces, and a cultural center, attracting both locals and tourists.
